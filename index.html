WHO WE ARE

OUR STORY

Tangent Technologies, founded in 2004, is dedicated to provide superior services in validation of computerized systems in regulated pharmaceutical industries, Process/Equipment Automation and Software development. Tangent Technologies is at forefront of providing best in-class Automation and validation services which are up-to-date with the continual regulatory changes. Tangent is a team of experienced engineers who with their expertise, commitment and tailored approach to the validation & automation needs of customers have a proven track record of providing quality services. Over a decade of experience in Process Automation coupled with software development gives us matchless expertise in computerized validation services (PLC/Lab Equipment Software/ERP/Automated Systems).Tangent brings to you the combination of technical expertise, hands-on experience, and regulatory knowledge needed to complete your validation project successfully.
